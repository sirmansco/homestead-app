🌿 Homestead
Private early access — thanks for testing

Hey! I'm testing a new app called Homestead — it's a private coordination tool for families who rely on a small circle of caregivers (grandparents, trusted sitters, family friends). Think of it as a shared calendar + bell system just for your care team, without the group-chat chaos.

I'd love your honest feedback as a real-world user. Here's how to get started:

Get started in 3 steps

1
Open the app Visit homestead-app-six.vercel.app on your phone. It works best in Safari on iPhone or Chrome on Android.
2
Sign in with Google Tap "Continue with Google" (or email) and create your account. It takes about 30 seconds. Use whichever email you'd want for this kind of app.
3
Add to your home screen (optional but recommended) In Safari: tap the Share icon → "Add to Home Screen." In Chrome: tap the three-dot menu → "Add to Home Screen." This makes it feel like a real app.

First time tip: After signing in you'll land on a household setup screen. Go ahead and create your household — give it a name (e.g. "The Smith House") and add a couple of kids. You can invite caregivers later from the Village tab.

What to poke around

📅
Almanac Your shift calendar. See open needs, claimed shifts, and who's covering.
➕
Post a Need Request coverage for a specific date/time. Add notes, pick which kids.
🔔
Ring the Bell One-tap SOS to your inner circle when you need help right now.
🏘️
Village Your care circle — Inner, Family, and Sitters. Tap the badge on a person's card to move them between circles.
🗓️
My Shifts If you join as a caregiver, see the shifts you've claimed and your schedule.
⏸️
Not Available Block out dates you're unavailable so parents can plan around you.

Known limitations right now

This is an early build. A few things are intentionally simple:

  • ⚠
    Caregiver preference in Post a Need — the "Notify" selector only appears once someone has joined your Village as a caregiver. If your Village is empty it won't show — that's expected.
  • ⚠
    Push notifications — supported on Android (Chrome) and desktop. On iPhone you'll need to add the app to your Home Screen first, then allow notifications when prompted. The bell icon also refreshes every 30 seconds while the app is open.

💡 Testing the caregiver side: Go to Village → tap the + button → choose "Invite via link." Copy the link and open it in an incognito window. Sign in with a second email — you'll land directly in the app as a caregiver. That gives you both a parent view and a real caregiver view side-by-side.
